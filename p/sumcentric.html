Download SumCentric


How To Play:

SumCentric is a free puzzle that involves logic and simple math.


Fill in the sections of the circle 1-31 (no repeats) to match the sums on the outside.
The 5 sections, in line, are added to the total.
Match all the totals to solve.


Outside the circle shows the sum & (amount left).


To Play:

Click any section and select an available number to place in the section.


Drag/zoom the game board for better usability.
Number box is draggable, as well.


Select the Options Button to:
Check Progress
Clear Board
Start New Game
View Games Solved
